Reviews

KR Creams has truly outdone themselves with their Lavender Honey Ice Cream! The delicate balance of floral lavender and rich honey creates a serene and delightful experience. Each scoop is like a gentle hug, perfect for warm evenings or a sweet treat after dinner. The smooth texture and elegant flavor profile make it stand out among other offerings. I highly recommend this unique flavor for anyone looking to elevate their ice cream game. It’s a lovely choice that pairs beautifully with fresh fruits or simply enjoyed on its own. Don’t miss out on this exquisite treat!
Tom Terryland
If you’re a fan of bold flavors, you must try KR Creams’ Spicy Dark Chocolate Ice Cream! The rich, indulgent dark chocolate is beautifully complemented by a surprising kick of chili that warms the palate with each bite. It’s a flavor adventure that challenges the norm, making it a perfect choice for those who love a bit of heat. The creamy texture combined with the complex flavor profile leaves you craving more. This ice cream is not just a dessert; it’s an experience! Pair it with a sprinkle of sea salt for an extra touch of perfection. I will for sure recommend this to all my friends.
Abel Nelson
KR Creams’ Mango Coconut Lime Ice Cream is a tropical delight that brings sunshine to any day! The combination of ripe mangoes, creamy coconut, and zesty lime creates a refreshing flavor that’s perfect for warm weather. Each scoop transports you to a beach paradise, making it the ultimate treat for summer gatherings or simply relaxing at home. The balance of sweetness and tanginess is spot on, making it enjoyable for everyone. I highly recommend this flavor for those looking to escape to a tropical paradise with every bite. It’s a must-try for any ice cream lover!
Derek C Volte
