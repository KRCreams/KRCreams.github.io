Butterfly Ice Cream

Butterfly Ice Cream by KR Creams is a whimsical frozen treat shaped like a butterfly. Despite its name, it doesn't contain any butterflies. The ice cream comes in a range of delicious flavors, from classic options like vanilla and chocolate to unique combinations like cookies and cream or caramel swirl. Its creamy texture and delightful shape make it a delightful and visually appealing dessert for any occasion.The butterfly shape of the ice cream adds an element of playfulness to the overall experience. The intricate details of the wings make each serving visually appealing, making it an ideal treat for special occasions or simply to enjoy on a sunny day.
